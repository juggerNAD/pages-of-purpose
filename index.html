About Dustin Ray

Dustin Ray is the founder of Pages of Purpose LLC. For more than a decade, he has combined research, practical legal understanding, and client-centered service to help individuals and families recover lost financial assets and resolve complex property situations.

At Pages of Purpose LLC, Dustin leads a small, focused team that performs detailed title and records research, liaises with agencies, and guides clients through each step until resolution. His approach is transparent: clear timelines, no hidden fees, and regular communication.

Dustin invests in educating communities about property rights and financial literacy, believing that informed people are better equipped to protect their interests.
